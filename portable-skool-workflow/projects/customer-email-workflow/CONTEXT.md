# Context

## What We Are Solving

Customer email work often spans threads, notes, documents, follow-ups, and internal handoff details. The workflow helps turn scattered information into clear summaries, reply drafts, and next actions.

## Desired Outcome

The agent helps produce:

- customer thread summaries
- missing-information lists
- concise reply drafts
- account or task status notes
- recommended next steps
- handoff notes when work moves from sales to operations

## What Good Looks Like

- Facts and recommendations are separated.
- Drafts are short and specific.
- Missing details are marked instead of guessed.
- The next step is clear.
- Sensitive source material stays local unless explicitly approved.

## What To Avoid

- Do not expose private customer details in shareable docs.
- Do not invent terms, prices, dates, or commitments.
- Do not modify mailboxes without confirmation.
- Do not include internal notes in customer-facing drafts.

## Simple Workflow

1. Intake: identify the customer, thread, task, and available source material.
2. Source check: review relevant local notes or documents.
3. Organize: separate facts, missing information, risks, and next steps.
4. Draft or advise: create the requested output.
5. Review: check for unsupported claims and unclear next actions.
